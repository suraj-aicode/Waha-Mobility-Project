Here's how you can take care/ increase the life of your Electric Scooter

Worried about the performance of your Electric Scooter?

Don’t stress! We’ve put together a simple list of do’s and don’ts to help you extend the battery life and overall performance of your electric scooter.

Every electric scooter has its own maintenance needs—and yours deserves special care. By following these easy tips, you’ll get the most out of your ride and proudly be part of the SmartRideRevolution!

Battery Care

Always use a damp cloth to clean the battery in case of dirt.

Never wash the battery with water.

Always charge the battery under shade or in a ventilated area.

It is not recommended to charge the battery under direct sunlight.

If not using the scooter, charge battery to 60% to preserve life.

Never dismantle the battery casing under any circumstances.

Charging

Connect your charger to a Standard (earthed) socket.

Never connect the charger in a non-standard (unearthed) socket.

Keep battery charge above 55V at all times.

Don’t ride if voltage drops below 53V.

Ensure scooter is OFF during charging.

Use the original charger only.

General

Respect the weight limit to protect the motor.

Don’t overload the scooter — it strains the motor.

Keep tires properly inflated to reduce battery drag.

Low pressure drains battery and reduces efficiency.

Pay attention to brakes, handlebars, and folding.
